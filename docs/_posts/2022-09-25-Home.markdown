I am very lucky to have grown up with a home garden. 
My home garden was one of the first places that got me interested in the natural world. 
I grew up around Hourglass treefrogs (_Polypydates cruciger_), Green garden lizards (_Calotes calotes_), and many other small but wonderful creatures. 
Here are some of them.
